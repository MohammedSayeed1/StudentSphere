Memory Game: Boost Your Brain Power!

Our Memory Game is designed to test your concentration and improve your memory by matching tiles featuring mental health images. The objective is simple: flip over two tiles at a time and try to find the matching pairs. The game continues until all the pairs are found. It's a fun, engaging way to sharpen your cognitive skills while raising awareness about mental health concepts.

How it works:
  • Start the game and you will see a set of face-down tiles with hidden images.
  • Click on two tiles to flip them over and reveal the images.
  • If the two images match, they will stay face-up. If not, they will flip back over after a brief moment.
  • Continue matching pairs until all tiles are uncovered.
  • Try to finish the game with the fewest moves and the quickest time!
